About us

Cobblerz: Care for Your Kicks.

Cobblerz, a small business founded in 2021 by friends at IIUM, is your go-to destination for all your footwear needs. We specialize in cleaning and repairing a wide range of shoes, from classic leather dress shoes to trendy sneakers and sports shoes.

Our expert team offers a variety of services, including deep cleaning to remove dirt and grime, expert repairs to fix broken soles or ripped seams, unyellowing treatments to restore the pristine white color of your sneakers, water repellent coatings to protect your shoes from water damage, deodorizing treatments to eliminate unpleasant odors, and customization options to add a personal touch.

Having already gained popularity among students and friends at IIUM, we're excited to expand our reach to a wider audience through our new website. Discover the Cobblerz difference and experience the transformation of your beloved footwear.
